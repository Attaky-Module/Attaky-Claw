For the breadboard form of Attaky Claw, you need one ESP32-S3 dev board, one or more breadboards, and optional extension peripherals.

## Required hardware

### ESP32-S3 dev board

To run Attaky Claw, you need an ESP32-S3 with **at least 8 MB Flash and 8 MB PSRAM**.

**We recommend an official ESP32-S3-DevKitC-1 board**. It exposes more pins and is suitable for breadboard assembly.
You can buy it from major electronics distributors.

### Breadboard

Prepare a breadboard with 400 tie points or more. It is recommended to reserve extra breadboards for optional peripherals.

### Wires and Dupont cables

Wires and Dupont jumper cables are needed for connections. Prepare enough quantity for wiring.

## Optional peripherals

Attaky Claw supports various extensions, including display, camera, microphone, and speaker.
These peripherals make interactions richer.

### Display

Attaky Claw supports external displays. The web flasher firmware is configured for a 2.0-inch SPI TFT color panel; a compatible model is linked below.

<LinkCard
    title="2.0-inch SPI TFT color display panel"
    href="https://item.taobao.com/item.htm?id=851909895424"
    description="Select: 2.0-inch, 8-pin blue board module, with header pins"
/>

If you choose another display, you may need software adaptation.

### Camera

Attaky Claw supports USB cameras for vision features. Even without a vision-capable model, captured images can still be sent to users.

For ESP32-S3, the USB camera should meet the requirements in the [USB Stream User Guide](https://docs.espressif.com/projects/esp-iot-solution/en/latest/usb/usb_host/usb_stream.html#id1).

<LinkCard
    title="USB camera on Taobao"
    href="https://item.taobao.com/item.htm?id=848914354287"
    description="This camera meets the requirements above"
/>

If you buy the recommended USB camera sample, it is recommended to also buy an MX1.25 4-pin single-ended cable (male) for breadboard wiring later.

### Microphone & speaker

Besides USB-UVC (USB camera), Attaky Claw also supports USB-UAC (microphone and speaker).

If you use the recommended USB camera sample, you only need to buy a microphone and speaker and connect them to the reserved ports on the camera with MX1.25 2-pin terminals.

### WS2812 light strip/ring

In addition to the built-in WS2812 LED on ESP32-S3-DevKitC-1, you can connect a WS2812 strip/ring to extend lighting effects.

Due to power limits, do not connect an overly long WS2812 strip/ring.
You can choose the WS2812 ring with the same model as the [demo image](https://detail.tmall.com/item.htm?id=994762490273&skuId=6139364670619).

### Servo

Attaky Claw supports servos for mechanical motion.
You can choose a common servo, such as [this one](https://item.taobao.com/item.htm?id=1016474722460).
